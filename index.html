Eta Omega Chi (HOX)

Epsilon Chapter – Babson College

Eta Omega Chi is the first and only Asian Professional Fraternity on the East Coast. We strive to build an elite community that shares exclusive resources and opportunities while pursuing entrepreneurship and building brotherhood that lasts a lifetime.

About HOX

HOX is built on the values of entrepreneurship, innovation, leadership, and cultural identity. Our mission is to encourage active leadership among peers, provide valuable professional development opportunities, and serve the community with a higher standard of social and cultural value.

What We Do

Professional Development

We host resume workshops, elevator pitch training sessions, business proposal development, and consulting case practice. These experiences prepare members for careers in finance, consulting, marketing, operations, accounting, and entrepreneurship.

Entrepreneurship

We promote the spirit of entrepreneurship by encouraging members to think creatively, take initiative, and develop innovative ideas. Members share academic resources and collaborate to grow professionally and personally.

Brotherhood

Beyond professional growth, HOX builds strong bonds between members through internal bonding events, mentorship, and shared experiences. We aim to create a lifelong network of support and friendship.

Executive Board

The Executive Board consists of the President, Senior Vice President, Chancellor, Executive External Director (PR), Executive Administrative Director, Executive Technology Director (Webmaster), Executive Professional Director (Alumni Relations), Executive Internal Director, and Executive Pledge Education Director.

Together, the board manages operations, professional programming, recruitment, marketing, finances, alumni relations, and brotherhood development.

Rush HOX

We look for students who demonstrate professionalism, strong character, and a genuine interest in business and entrepreneurship. Applicants must show commitment, initiative, and the ability to contribute positively to our community.

The rush process typically includes an information session, professional event, social event, deliberations, and bid decisions. Selected candidates enter the pledge process before becoming full members.

Alumni Network

HOX alumni remain part of our lifelong network after graduation. Alumni continue to support the fraternity through mentorship, professional guidance, and continued community engagement.
